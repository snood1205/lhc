1. 1 + 1 and 2: They are equivalent expressions
  2. 10 ^ 2 and 10 + 9 * 10. These are also equivalent. 10 + 9 * 10 is equivalent to 10 + 90 which is the same as 10 ^ 2 as they are both 100.
  3. 400 - 37 is not equivalent to (-) 37 400. (-) 37 400 is equivalent to 37 - 400 which is the opposite of 400 - 37.
  4. 100 `div` 3 is not the same as 100 / 3. The first is integral division and will return 33 whereas the second is normal division and will return 33.33333333.
  5. 2 * 5 + 18 is not the same as 2 * (5 + 18) The first is 10 + 18 = 28 whereas the second is 2 * 23 = 46.
